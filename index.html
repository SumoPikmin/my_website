About Me

I'm a second-year Master's student at the Technical University of Darmstadt specializing in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the Technical University of Darmstadt. As part of my studies, I completed two exchange semesters abroad — at Högskolan i Skövde, Sweden (January 2023 – June 2023) and National Taiwan University, Taiwan (February 2025 – June 2025) — gaining international academic and cultural experience.

Projects

  • Bachelor's Thesis: Evaluating Cultural Diversity in Text-to-Image Models
    (June 2024 – December 2024)

    I explored how well Text-to-Image (T2I) models reflect cultural diversity by creating a multilingual benchmark with culturally rich concepts and complex prompts. I developed a Python and LLM-based data pipeline and introduced a new evaluation metric using multimodal models.

  • Traffic Management System: Incident Module
    (Oct 2023 – March 2024)

    As part of a university software project, I contributed to building an incident module for a railway traffic management system. I focused on backend logic in Python, UI development with Tkinter, and quality assurance, while applying agile methods and collaborative Git workflows.
